About Me

I am an experienced maths tutor based in Chinnor, Oxfordshire, working with all ages up to GCSE higher, with good results.

A graduate in mathematics from Oxford University, I spent many years as an airline pilot before starting a family and returning to my favourite school subject. I thoroughly enjoy helping people feel more confident in maths - I'm saddened when people say they can’t do it, as I strongly believe that with a little help and encouragement they absolutely can!
